Kick-off meeting

Our consortium partners for the Horizon Europe project MULTHEM, participated in the kick-off meeting at Sittard and Geleen in The Netherlands. The meeting was hosted by Brightlands Materials Center over two days on 12 and 13 January 2023. MULTHEM aims to develop polymer-metal-based materials and lightweight structures with good thermal management using Additive Manufacturing (AM) and innovative joining technologies to achieve complex freeform geometries. The kick-off meeting was a great start to our 3 year project!
