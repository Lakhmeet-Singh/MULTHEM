Nanotechnology is the science of manipulating matter on an atomic, molecular, and supramolecular scale. Integration of both these domains can create wide variety of materials for new and innovative applications.

MULTHEM

The aim of the MULTHEM project is to develop polymer-metal-based new materials and lightweight structures with good thermal management using Additive Manufacturing (AM) and innovative joining technologies.
